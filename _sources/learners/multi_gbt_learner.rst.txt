MultiGBTLearner
===============

A multi-model extension of GBTLearner supporting several independent learners, each with its own parameters and optimizer. Useful in actor-critic settings or multi-head architectures.
It supports training, prediction, saving/loading, and SHAP value computation per ensemble.
